Bonna SmartTrack

Bonna is an autonomous Price Tracker, an innovative tool designed to enhance your online shopping experience. Through automatic pushed alerts and a user-friendly interface the main goal is to save both time and money.
This intelligent tracker simplifies the process of monitoring product prices, providing you with real-time updates and valuable insights. With accurate and timely data gathered through web scraping techniques, the price tracker ensures you stay informed about price fluctuations across various e-commerce platforms.
Offering historical price analysis, you can gain valuable insights into price trends, seasonal fluctuations, and optimal buying periods.
