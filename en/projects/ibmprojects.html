IBM Projects

During my tenure as a Full Stack Developer at IBM, I have had the opportunity to contribute to several groundbreaking AlphaBank projects that have reshaped the industry.
These projects spanned diverse areas, including digital banking, risk assessment, and payment processing.
Through collaborative teamwork and the collective expertise of our diverse team, we played a pivotal role in crafting innovative solutions that empower this financial institution to thrive in the digital age. By pooling our technical skills and knowledge, we successfully delivered transformative projects that enhance efficiency, security, and customer experience.
Some of the most highlighted and commonly used applications in AlphaBank are the following :

  • MyAlpha Documents, supporting documents transaction between Customers and Bank operators, digital signatures and digital seals, automatic processes to complete Customer transaction, investment, insurance, cards applications
  • AlphaBank eGov-KYC integration, supporting automatic update of Customer's personal details online through mobile or computer
  • Consumer Loans and Cards requests, supporting automatic approval of Microlending or new card request in less than 5 minutes
  • Business / Retail on Boarding, supporting starting new business or personal account and e-Banking credentials at Alpha Bank through computer or mobile.
